Line Follower Bot

This is a obstacle avoiding line follower bot. This is a group project by 3 members for our robotics club roboism.In this project, we used an Arduino Uno to make an obstacle avoiding line follower bot which uses 2 IR sensors to detect the black line to follow and an ultrasonic sensor to check for the obstacle and it stops if it encounters an object, within a particular range, in the front of the bot. IR sensor sends signals and receives the bounced back / reflected signals and detects whether it is black path to follow or other path to change , as the black colour absorbs most of the light signals. For detecting obstacles we used an ultrasonic sensor which works on the same principle and gives us the distance between the bot and obstacle . Hence we can stop and wait till it gets out of our path.
